1. About DPG School of Technology & Management

DPG School of Technology & Management (DPG STM) is a higher education institution located in Gurugram, Haryana. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Applications, Management, Commerce, and related disciplines, and is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak.

This website is an unofficial, student-created informational page developed for learning, academic support, and blogging purposes. It does not represent the official administration or authority of the institution. To learn more about this institute , Kindly refers to the Homepage of DPG STM.

2. Academic Environment & Programs

DPG STM (School of Technology and Management) offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in management and computer applications, including MBA, MCA, BBA, BCA, and B.Tech (with specializations like AI & ML, Data Science). Affiliated with MDU Rohtak, DPG STM provides technical education, vocational training, and short-term certification courses in areas like Financial Planning, Business Analytics, and Full Stack Development, aiming for practical skills alongside traditional degrees.

The campus supports classroom learning, internal assessments, practical labs, seminars, and university examinations conducted under MDU guidelines.

3. Campus Life & Learning Culture

Student life at DPG STM reflects a balance between academics, skill-building, and personal growth. Regular lectures, practical sessions, internal tests, and project work form the foundation of academic routines.

This page also serves as a personal blog space where academic tools, front-page generators, and learning utilities are shared to support students during coursework and submissions.
